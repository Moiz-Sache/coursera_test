Corona virus Info

How it spreads?

People can catch COVID-19 from others who have the virus. The disease can spread from person to person through small droplets from the nose or mouth which are spread when a person with COVID-19 coughs or exhales. These droplets land on objects and surfaces around the person.

Symptoms

The main symptoms of corona virus include: fever,coughing,shortness of breath,body ache,headache,fatigue,soar throat,nosia,diarrhea,loss of smell or taste.The virus can lead to pneumonia,respiratory failure ,septic shock and death.

Precautions


Clean your hands often,use soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub.Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.Don't touch your eyes,nose or mouth.Follow the directions of your local health authority.
